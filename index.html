The Adventures of Winston

Meet Winston, a very brave corgi who needs to collect all the bones from the backyard. However, the pesky squirrels keep getting in his way. Collect all the bones before the squirrels catch Winston. However, if Winston eats some chicken, the squirrels turn into squeaky balls and he can chase them down for some extra points!

Click start and use your arrow keys to navigate Winston around the board!
